Status: Work In Progress

About: This small project of mine is to be a custom-built speaker, inspired by the iconic design of the radio from Valve's Portal. This will feature a 3D printed case and Bluetooth connectivity, where I am able to play any music from my phone directly to the speaker. A small LED screen will be used to display the design's "radio frequency", or alternatively display the song currently being played - all controlled by an Arduino. This will be powered via Li-ion batteries - though I may replace this with a rechargable battery pack instead. When turned on, the speaker will glow in blue from LED lights concealed behind translucent materials.
